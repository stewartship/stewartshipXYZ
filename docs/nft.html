Marawunder

Marawunder (Mara Carmona, Barceolna, ES) is a scientist (microbiologist) and an artist who loves nature. Passionate about divulgation, her artwork NaturaOnirica is an example of her desire to create a bridge between art and science in which both disciplines are combined to give voice to the nature. @NaturaOnirica

CEVEN Collection

CEVEN works on the creation of a Governance-as-a-Service for natural ecosystems. When participating in the SNI Experimental Zone Event at De Ceuvel, they produced a series of NFTs out of various data sources (images, sensor data, satellite data) to support the De Ceuvel cause.

Jack Julian

Jack Julian (The Netherlands) - Audio/Visual artist. Jack has been exploring creativity, and experimenting with different forms of expression since his early years. With nature as his first inspiration. For this event he has been using AI tools to (re) create, by using photos of De Ceuvel. He believes AI can teach us more about, and (re)connect us with nature.
