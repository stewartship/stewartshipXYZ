stewartship is Soft Launching at De Ceuvel 8 Jaar

stewartship presents an exclusive art collection that supports the cause of De Ceuvel. Every artwork is for sale as a digital piece, and the proceeds are split between the artist, De Ceuvel, and Sovereign Nature Initiative, which acts as an enabler. By acquiring artworks, you support three causes! The artworks of Marawunder (ES), Jack Julain (NL), and others are sold as hybrid or digital copies at De Ceuvel and online. Talk to us! Also, we are happy to educate you about the latest developments in digital artwork, ownership, and how we facilitate the royalties distribution. We use leading-edge technology (Non-Fungible Tokens, “NFTs”) and are happy to be inspired by where our experimental approach leads us; maybe you are the inspiration we’ve been looking for. 

The artworks sold for the De Ceuvel 8 Jaar event generate money for the De Ceuvel ecosystem and by that support its further existance. Explore the artists and their artwoks.

Tweet a nature related photo from De Ceuvel and win tokenized carbon credits (forestry (REDD+) carbon credits) to offset your next journey!

What is stewartship

stewartship curates and explores artists that work on the intersection of art, technology, and nature conservation

We explore ways to create stewardship in a natural ecosystem by creating artistic value out of data about the ecosystem (sensor data, images, earth observation) or inspiration by conservation efforts.
